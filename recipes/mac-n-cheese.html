Mac and Cheese Recipe

Photo: AllRecipes

Description

This is the macaroni with the cheeeeeeeese! A this dish of rich and velvety cheese makes a perfect side for dinner or even a nice lunch. Anyone who is able to resist this dish shouldn't be trusted!

This recipe requires 10 min of prep time and 45 min of cook time. This make the total time from start to finish for this recipe 55 min. This dish serves up to four people.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni: Grab a box of your favorite macaroni noodles.
  • Butter & Flower: You'll need butter and flour to make the rich and creamy cheese sauce.
  • Milk:Whole make is preferred.
  • Seasonings:Salt, pepper, and paprika.
  • Bread crumbs:Use store-bought dried bread crumbs or make your own.

How to Mak Mac and Cheese Step-By-Step

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Make the cheese sauce
  3. Make the topping
  4. Bake the mac and cheese
